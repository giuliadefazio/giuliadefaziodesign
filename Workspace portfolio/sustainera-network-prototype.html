Our Parts

ORIGINAL ENGINEERING.
SECOND DEPLOYMENT.

Manufacturing a new part produces up to 80% more CO² than regenerating one. Sustainera takes original Stellantis components and unlocks their full potential, through four principles that turn end-of-life into back-in-play.

REMAN

Fully disassembled and rebuilt to original specifications.

REPAIR

Precision repair where the component allows it.

REUSE

Validated components reintegrated without modification.

RECYCLE

What can't be saved fuels the next cycle.
